# Morning and Evening - Markdown Edition

This project brings *"Morning and Evening"* by the renowned 19th-century British preacher Charles Spurgeon—widely regarded as the "Prince of Preachers"—to the digital age in an easy-to-use, markdown-optimized format. Based on Spurgeon's classic devotional, this version is designed to help you experience the timeless wisdom, comfort, and theological insight of *Morning and Evening* every day of the year, including leap years with February 29th.

## Project Features

Each entry contains:

- *A Scripture Verse:* One verse or short passage to begin your day or reflection.
- *Morning & Evening Meditations:* Spurgeon's insightful essays (200–400 words) exploring the meaning and application of the verse.
- *A Christ-centered focus:* Directly connecting every message to the life, teachings, and significance of Jesus Christ.

## Why Markdown?

- ✅ *Portability:* View your devotions on any device without losing formatting.
- ✅ *Organization:* Quickly find and navigate by date, theme, or passage.
- ✅ *Customization:* Easily add highlights, notes, or bookmarks for deeper engagement.
- ✅ *Version Control:* Track changes, contribute, or collaborate with others if desired.
- ✅ *Easy to Share:* Publish or sync with note-taking apps and journals.
- ✅ *Accessibility:* Markdown is compatible with most modern software and assistive technologies.

## Structure and Content

- *Yearly Calendar:* The devotional is arranged by each date, including special days like leap years.
- *Two Entries Per Day:* Each day provides one morning and one evening reading.
- *Scripture-Based:* Every section starts with a verse or passage from the Bible.
- *Theologically Rich:* Spurgeon blends deep doctrinal insight with practical application.
- *Puritan & Poetic Style:* Beautiful, reflective writing that is both profound and relatable.

## Usage

Clone or download the repository to access the Markdown files. Open them in any Markdown viewer or editor to read or repurpose the content.
